## Architectures

### Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs)
- **LeNet-5**: The pioneer of digit recognition.
- **AlexNet**: The breakthrough in ImageNet.
- **ResNet**: Solving the vanishing gradient with residual connections.

### Modern Architectures
- **EfficientNet**: Scaling depth, width, and resolution.
- **Vision Transformers (ViT)**: Applying self-attention to image patches.
